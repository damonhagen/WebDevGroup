Why Caustic?



    Why choose Caustic 3?
  • Caustic 3 has multiple synthesizers to choose from
  • Various Sound to mix and choose mainly electro music/dubstep music
  • Optimized for mobile devices
  • Effects rack supporting 2 effects per machine. (20 effect types)
  • Mixer desk with global Delay/Reverb effect
  • Master section with parametric EQ and limiter
  • Song sequencer
  • Functional interface which makes all the features of the app clear
  • the rack-mount style machines are set out vertically, and can be scrolled between.
  • solid recording capabilities built in, as well as audiobus and audio-copy on IOS.
